Standia is an AI agency built around one core idea: your phone should never go unanswered. We deploy custom AI voice agents that handle inbound calls on behalf of businesses — dentist offices, gyms, law firms, service companies — any organisation that loses value every time a call goes to voicemail or a receptionist is unavailable.

Each agent is trained on the client's specific workflows: booking appointments, answering FAQs, qualifying leads, routing urgent calls to the right person. The result is a seamless caller experience that operates 24/7, at a fraction of the cost of a human receptionist.
